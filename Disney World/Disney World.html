The Beginning

Ever since it was founded in 1971, Disney World has been providing entertainment to families. Located in Orlando, Florida, Disney World is home to four theme parks: Magic Kingdom, Epcot, Hollywood Studios, and Animal Kingdom (all founded in different years). There are also two water parks: Typhoon Lagoon and Blizzard Beach.

A Work in Progress

Disney World hasn't always been the multi-amusement park powerhouse that we know it as today. Each separate park within the property was founded in a different year. Check out our timeline to see the evolution of Disney World!
